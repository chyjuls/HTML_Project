About me...



My full name is Chinyere Ugorji, but friends and colleagues know me as Chichi.
I currently work within the healthcare sector but have a passion for all things technology, which influenced to a large extent, my decision to pursue a course in Diagnostic radiography.
At that time I was interested in patient care as well as technology and sought to integrate both.
In current times, my passion for technology has outweighed my love for healthcare, although I know based on current projections there is a place for technology in medicine and healthcare.
I have done some self-learning in the different aspects of information technology and have been overwhelmed at the diversity within the industry.


My Tech Journey…..

I started out with hardware, having taken exams in CompTIA networking and Microsoft’s foundation exams in networking.
I have dipped in and out of databases(SQL), data analytics, business analysis and project management,
this left me confused for a while until I stumbled on a personality test online, which showed that I liked taking up new challenges.
The personality test also showed I was a problem solver.
This helped me evaluate and stream-line my choices, with regards to a career in information technology.

Women in Cyber Academy in Focus.

I have finally decided to choose the Cybersecurity path. To help me understand more, I did some research online and did some free and paid online courses in Cybersecurity. Some of this courses cost me a lot of money and some of the course providers I figured, were only after my money. Then I heard about Women in Cyber academy.

My journey with WIC (Women in Cyber academy), thus began this year after a phone interview. So far my journey towards fulfilling my dream is on course and I must say, I am enjoying every moment of it. I have given myself to learning programing and have been watching videos on end. I hope that one day, I will use my skills to make an immense contribution to the healthcare industry.
